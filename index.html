Our Work

Our mission is to provide our customers with a user-friendly servive that will help them identify which streaming service option will fit them the best. Our website shows the contents of each service and what they have to offer. We have also included sample movies and TV shows that will be on the website. You can also explore what each movie and TV show have to offer and search where your favorites will be.

Netflix

Pros

  • Excellent Selection
  • Good Recommentdations

Cons

  • More Expensive
  • Movies don't stay on forever
  • Limited New Content

Outlook:

Considered the best streaming service in the business, no other service has been able to match Netflix's success. One of the perks is that you can create up to five different profiles so you can share it with family and friends and stay on the same plan.

Hulu

Pros

  • Good for Current TV
  • Cheap
  • Student Discount

Cons

  • Commercials
  • Large Interface
  • Inconsistent TV Selections

Outlook:

This provides a good alternative for people who don't want cable but like to keep up with current TV shows. This service provides access to most major network shows and a handful of cable shows the day after they air.

HBO NOW

Pros

  • Award-winning Programming
  • Original News Programming
  • Top-tier Movies/TV Shows

Cons

  • More Expensive

Outlook:

This service is built upon the high quality of their originial TV shows, and is the same as having the cable channel itself.

Amazon Prime Video

Pros

  • Good Original Shows
  • Included with Other Amazon Benefits
  • Student Discount

Cons

  • Rarely Gets New Shows
  • Not Available on Some Devices

Outlook:

Prime offers movies, TV shows, and original programming, as well as free two-day shipping on Prime purchases. Customers also recieve a free monthly Kindle book and have access to Amazon Music and Amazon Prime Instant Video.
